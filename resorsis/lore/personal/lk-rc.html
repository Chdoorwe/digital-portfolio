Summary

Subject KENNEDY is an elite operative of the Secure Projects Force, specializing in anomaly suppression, civilian protection, and high‑risk extraction missions. His Ω‑2 rank places him among the most trusted and capable individuals remaining after the Epsilon Event.

The Lost Archive Council considers KENNEDY a stabilizing force across the fractured territories, with multiple enclaves citing his actions as the sole reason for their continued survival.

Epsilon Event Context

The Epsilon Event resulted in the collapse of global infrastructure and the loss of approximately 90% of humanity. In the aftermath, the Secure Projects Force was tasked with identifying, containing, and eliminating emergent anomalies threatening the remaining population.

KENNEDY volunteered for frontline deployment during the earliest breach cycles. His efforts directly enabled the evacuation of over ███ civilians across the Outer Shards during the Collapse Phase.
